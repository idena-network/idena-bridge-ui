The bridge uses a digital vault which holds iDNA coins on Idena blockchain and issue wiDNA tokens pegged to iDNA 1:1 on Binance Smart Chain. The bridge is hosted by Idena Core Team.
Bridge vault is the owner of your Idena coins. And you are the owner of Wrapped Idena coins because coins are sent to your BSC address.
You can view reserves on Idena vualt && BSC contract
You can trade iDNA on any decentralized exchange on Binance Smart Chain (e.g. Pancake Swap) or hold it on any wallet which supports BEP-20 token standard (e.g. Trust Wallet)
