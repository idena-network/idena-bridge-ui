Wrapped iDNA is a cryptocurrency token pegged to the value of the iDNA coin. Itâ€™s called wrapped because the original asset is put in a wrapper, a kind of a digital vault that allows the wrapped version to be created on another blockchain.
The bridge uses a digital vault which holds iDNA coins on Idena blockchain and issue wiDNA tokens on the binance smart chain that is pegged at a 1:1 ratio to iDNA native coin. The bridge is hosted by the Idena Core Team.
Bridge vault is the owner of your Idena coins. And you are the owner of Wrapped Idena coins because coins are sent to your BSC address.
You can see reserves on Idena vualt & BSC contract .
You can trade iDNA on any decentralized exchange on Binance Smart Chain (e.g. Pancake Swap) or hold it on any wallet which supports BEP-20 token standard (e.g. Trust Wallet).
